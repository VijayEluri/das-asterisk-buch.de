  -= Info about application 'OSPAuth' =-

[Synopsis]
OSP authentication

[Description]
  OSPAuth([provider[,options]]):  Authenticate a SIP INVITE by OSP and sets
the variables:
 ${OSPINHANDLE}:  The inbound call transaction handle
 ${OSPINTIMELIMIT}:  The inbound call duration limit in seconds

This application sets the following channel variable upon completion:
        OSPAUTHSTATUS   The status of the OSP Auth attempt as a text string, one of
                SUCCESS | FAILED | ERROR

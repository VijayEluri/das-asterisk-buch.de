  -= Info about application 'OSPFinish' =-

[Synopsis]
Record OSP entry

[Description]
  OSPFinish([status[,options]]):  Records call state for ${OSPINHANDLE}, according to
status, which should be one of BUSY, CONGESTION, ANSWER, NOANSWER, or CHANUNAVAIL
or coincidentally, just what the Dial application stores in its ${DIALSTATUS}.

This application sets the following channel variable upon completion:
        OSPFINISHSTATUS The status of the OSP Finish attempt as a text string, one of
                SUCCESS | FAILED | ERROR
